Thermal stability (ARC)
[FiveVB, Wolfram Kohs]
Test intention
This test aims on determining the thermal stability limits of lithium-ion cells. A nearly identical
testing procedure is found in FreedomCar:2005 and SAE 2464:2009.
Applications
The test results allow the definition of a safety temperature limit of a cell / the onset temperature
of a thermal runaway. Also, the risk potential of a cell can be evaluated. The results also allow
comparisons between different cell chemistries and cell manufacturers.
Test approach
Fully charged cells are slowly heated up until venting and later a thermal runaway occurs.
Mentionable is the approach not only to test new cells, but also cells at mid-life and EOL. Also,
overcharged cells if still working can be tested.
