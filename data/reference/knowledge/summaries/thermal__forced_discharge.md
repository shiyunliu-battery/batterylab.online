Forced discharge
[FiveVB, Wolfram Kohs]
Test intention
The purpose of this test is to verify the behaviour of a cell, and if there, the performance of over-
discharge protection devices in case of deep discharge caused by BMS failure or similar. The test is
applied on single cells, there are tests with similar intentions for modules and packs.
Application(s)
The results allow an estimation of a cell´s hazard potential. Also, an evaluation of different cell
chemistries, cell designs, and safety devices is possible.
